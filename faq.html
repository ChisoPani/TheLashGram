What are eyelash extension?

Eyelash extensions are semi-permanent extensions comprised of individual synthetic polyester eyelashes that are curved to replicate a natural eyelash. They are applied to individual eyelashes, one extension at a time, for a natural, elegant look to lengthen and add fullness to the existing lashes.

What Is the procedure like?

The application of a full set of lash extensions is a safe, painless, relaxing procedure that takes between 60minutes to 1hr 30 minutes to apply. You will be comfortably reclined with your eyes closed.

How long do lash extensions last?

The look can be maintained indefinitely with refills. When applied, eyelash extensions will remain in place until the natural lash one which it rests falls out. Natural lashes grow and fall out in cycles every (60-70) days. Please note that this is a perfectly normal occurrence, with or without extensions. Lash touch-ups are necessary every two or three weeks in order to maintain your look.

Will lash extensions damage my natural lashes?

NO. In fact, lash extensions are kinder to your natural lashes because you don’t curl or apply mascara, which can dry out and even break your natural eyelashes. Lash extensions look and feel completely real.

Can I wear mascara?

The main advantage of eyelash extensions is that you really don’t need to wear mascara.
